JC's Favorite Movies of All Time

These are 3 of my favorite movies of all time as of November 2023

There Will Be Blood

This Paul Thomas Anderson film is a masterpiece character study of a budding oil baron's greediness and relentlessness to win.

12 Years a Slave

The true story of Solomon Northrup, a free man captured and sold into slavery in the American South, that is one of the grandest audio-visual experiences to behold.

Parasite

An incredible, unique story from Bong Joon-ho. Masterful characters and an intense sense of place from one of the greatest directors of all time.
